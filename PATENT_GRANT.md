This repository is licensed under the **Apache License, Version 2.0** (see `LICENSE`),
which includes an **explicit, irrevocable patent grant** in **Section 3 ("Grant of Patent
License")**. This file states that grant plainly so adopters and contracting authorities
can verify it without reading the full licence.

## What you receive
Each contributor grants you a **perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge,
royalty-free, irrevocable patent licence** to make, use, sell, offer to sell, import and
otherwise transfer this work, for any patent claims they own or control that are
necessarily infringed by their contribution alone or by its combination with this work.

## Defensive termination
If you initiate patent litigation (including a cross-claim or counterclaim) alleging that
this work, or a contribution incorporated in it, constitutes direct or contributory patent
infringement, **any patent licences granted to you under Apache-2.0 for this work terminate**
as of the date such litigation is filed (Apache-2.0 §3, final sentence).

## Why this matters (COM(2026)503)
AI Act deployers inherit liability for the compliance code they are legally required to run.
A bare copyright licence (MIT/BSD/ISC is silent on patents) gives them **no patent immunity**.
The Apache-2.0 §3 grant here gives downstream users enforceable patent peace on this code,
and removes later patent assertion as an exclusionary lever against the commons.

We recommend the Commission make **an OSI licence carrying an explicit patent grant** a
baseline eligibility condition for EU-funded, endorsed, or procured open-source building
blocks.
